# Example: Stravinsky Rhythm Engine — 4-Bar Displaced Accents

## Input

rhythmic_material: 4 quarter notes per bar (4/4)
metre: 4/4

## Parameters

displacement_strength: 0.7
block_rhythm_bias: 0.6

## Output

Accent map: Accents on "and" of 1, "and" of 2, "and" of 3, "and" of 4 (each bar)
Rhythmic cells: 2-note cell (eighth + eighth) repeated; accents on second eighth
Metric tension: Beat implied by metre; accents on offbeats create conflict

Conceptual rhythm (x = accent):
|  x   x   x   x  |
|  &   &   &   &  |
1  2  3  4  1  2  3  4  ...

## Notes

Classic Stravinsky displacement. The metre is 4/4 but accents land on the "and" of each beat. Block character: repeated 2-note cell. The beat is felt but contradicted. Useful for jazz comping, contemporary percussion, or rhythmic backgrounds in arranging.
