# Example: Jimmy Wyble Engine — 2:1 Voice Ratio

## Harmonic Context

Dm7 | G7 | Cmaj7 (4 bars)

## Output (Conceptual)

**Upper line:** D4 E4 F4 | G4 A4 G4 | F4 E4 D4 | C4 D4 E4 | C4

**Lower line:** A3 — | D3 — | C3 — | G3 — | E3

## How It Works

- **2:1 ratio:** Upper voice has two notes per lower-voice note. voice_ratio_mode: two_to_one.
- **Lower on strong beats:** Lower voice attacks on beats 1 and 3; upper fills with stepwise motion.
- **Contrary motion:** Upper ascends over Dm7; lower descends A→D. Opposite directions.
- **Implied harmony:** Dm7 (D–A, E–A), G7 (G–D, A–D), Cmaj7 (C–G, E–E). Clear without block voicings.

## Notes

Wyble explicitly uses 2:1 and 3:1 ratios. More systematic than 1:1; good for exercise mode.
