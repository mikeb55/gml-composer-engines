# Example 01: Contemporary Counterpoint — Three-Line Chamber Fragment

## Input

- harmonic_context: Am7–D7–Gmaj7
- line_count: 3
- dissonance_level: 0.5
- intervallic_cell_bias: 0.6
- overlap_tolerance: 0.5

## Output

Three lines. Voice 1: M2–m3–P4 cell, resolves bar 4. Voice 2: continues through bar 4 (phrase_overlap). Voice 3: hands off to Voice 1 at bar 3. Registers complementary. Dissonance within tolerance.

## Notes

Harmony emerges from line interaction. Suitable for guitar trio or chamber ensemble.
