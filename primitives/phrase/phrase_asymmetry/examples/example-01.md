## Parameters

metre: 4/4
bar_count: 8

## Notes

Classic 3+3+2 over 8 bars. The 2-bar tail creates a short "kick" that disrupts square expectation. Common in Latin jazz and post-bop. The phrase resolves on bar 8 but the grouping makes bar 6 feel like a mini-cadence, with bars 7–8 as a compressed response.
