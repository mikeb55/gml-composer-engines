# Notes: Line Handoff

## Rationale

Contemporary counterpoint often requires one line to yield melodic prominence to another. Unlike block-chord writing, lines hand off responsibility so texture remains fluid.

## Design Decisions

- Handoff point can be phrase-boundary or mid-phrase.
- Continuity is preserved: the yielding line may sustain, rest, or provide minimal support.
- Avoid all voices yielding simultaneously.
