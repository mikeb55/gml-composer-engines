# Example: Line Handoff — Two-Line Fragment

## Input

Two voices, phrase boundary at bar 4.

## Output

Voice 1: active bars 1–4, yields at bar 4
Voice 2: enters bar 4, takes lead through bar 8

## Notes

Voice 1 sustains or rests as Voice 2 takes over. No abrupt break. The handoff primitive ensures melodic responsibility transfers smoothly without texture collapse.
