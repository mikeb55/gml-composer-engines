# Notes: Rhythmic Independence

## Rationale

Contemporary counterpoint requires voices to have distinct rhythmic identities. Lockstep motion reduces texture to homophony.

## Design Decisions

- Staggered attacks and offsets create independence.
- Rhythmic offset bias controls how much voices diverge.
- Syncopation and displacement support distinct profiles.
