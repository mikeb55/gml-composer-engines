# Notes: Dissonance Tolerance

## Rationale

Contemporary counterpoint allows more vertical dissonance than Bach. This primitive controls how much tension is acceptable.

## Design Decisions

- Dissonance level 0–1: 0 = consonance-focused, 1 = liberal dissonance.
- Seconds, sevenths, tritones allowed at higher levels.
- Resolves or sustains controlled by context.
