# Example: Intervallic Cell Counterpoint — Two-Line Fragment

## Input

Two voices, cell seed [M2, m3, P4].

## Output

Voice 1: M2–m3–P4 cell, transposed
Voice 2: P4–M2–m3 cell, inverted

## Notes

Cells recur and transform. Harmony emerges from interaction. The primitive builds counterpoint from intervallic logic rather than block chords.
