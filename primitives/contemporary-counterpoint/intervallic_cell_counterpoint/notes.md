# Notes: Intervallic Cell Counterpoint

## Rationale

Contemporary counterpoint often builds from intervallic cells (M2, m3, P4) rather than chord tones alone. Cells recur and transform.

## Design Decisions

- Intervallic cell bias: 0 = chord-tone focus, 1 = cell-driven.
- Cell seed defines initial cell set.
- Harmony can emerge from cell interaction.
