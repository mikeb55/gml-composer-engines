# Example: Register Interlock — Two-Line Fragment

## Input

Two voices, 4 bars.

## Output

Voice 1: primary zone E4–G5, brief cross to A3 at bar 3
Voice 2: primary zone C3–E4, brief cross to F4 at bar 2

## Notes

Voices occupy complementary zones. Brief crossing allowed; texture remains clear. Register interlock ensures clarity even when lines overlap momentarily.
