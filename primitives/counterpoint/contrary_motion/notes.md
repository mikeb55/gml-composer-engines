# Notes: Contrary Motion

## Rationale

Contrary motion creates independence and clarity. When one voice rises, the other falls; the ear separates them. Bach and Fux prioritise contrary motion.

## Design Decisions

- "Prefers" allows occasional parallel or similar motion for stylistic reasons.
- Strength parameter can tune how strongly contrary motion is enforced.
