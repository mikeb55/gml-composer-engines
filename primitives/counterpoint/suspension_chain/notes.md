# Notes: Suspension Chain

## Rationale

Suspensions (4–3, 7–6, 9–8) are core to Bach counterpoint. Preparation–suspension–resolution. The primitive generates chains that can repeat across voices.

## Design Decisions

- Dissonance must be prepared (same pitch in prior beat).
- Resolution is always downward by step.
- suspension_types can specify 4–3, 7–6, 9–8, etc.
