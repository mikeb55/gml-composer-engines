# Notes: Oblique Motion

## Rationale

Oblique motion—one voice holds, the other moves—is essential for suspensions and pedal points. It anchors harmony while allowing melodic motion.

## Design Decisions

- The sustained voice can be specified or inferred from context.
- Stepwise motion preferred in the moving voice; leaps allowed for variety.

## References

- Bach, chorales and inventions
- Species counterpoint (oblique as one of four motion types)
