# Example: Dyad Density Control — Two-Line Fragment

## Input

Two voices, 4 beats.

## Output

Beat 1: Upper E4 (line only)
Beat 2: Upper F4 + Lower C3 (dyad)
Beat 3: Lower D3 (line only)
Beat 4: Upper G4 + Lower E3 (dyad)

## Notes

Texture alternates: line → dyad → line → dyad. The primitive supports playable two-line motion by avoiding continuous double-stops and allowing breathing room between dyads. Dyadic emphasis can occur at cadences.
