# Notes: Dyad Density Control

## Rationale

Two-line guitar writing alternates single-line motion with dyads. Constant double-stops are fatiguing and reduce melodic clarity; constant single-line loses the two-voice character. This primitive controls the balance.

## Design Decisions

- Default pattern: line → dyad → line → dyad.
- Structural moments (cadences, phrase ends) can emphasise dyads.
- Avoids continuous double-stop texture.
