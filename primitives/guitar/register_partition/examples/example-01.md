# Example: Register Partition — Two-Line Fragment

## Input

Two voices for solo guitar.

## Output

Upper voice: E4 F4 G4 A4
Lower voice: C3 D3 E3 F3

## Notes

Upper voice stays in treble (E4–A4); lower in bass (C3–F3). Clear register separation. The primitive supports playable two-line motion by keeping voices in distinct zones, avoiding muddiness and impossible stretches. Momentary crossing (e.g. lower A3 above upper G4) allowed within tolerance.
