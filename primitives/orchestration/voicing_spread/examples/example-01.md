# Example: Voicing Spread — Open 4-Way

## Input

chord: Cmaj7
spread_spec: { spread: open, density: 4, bass_separation: true }

## Parameters

register_constraint: C3–C6

## Output

Voicing (bottom to top):
- C2 (bass, separated)
- G3, B3, E4 (open position: root, 3rd, 7th in upper structure)
- Or: C3, E3, G3, B4 (open with root in bass of upper structure)

## Notes

Open voicing creates space between chord tones. Bass separation puts root in low register; upper voices spread across G3–B4 or similar. Typical for sax section or mixed brass. Avoids muddiness in low register. Ellington-style openness.
