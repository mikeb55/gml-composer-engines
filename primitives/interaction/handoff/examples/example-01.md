# Example: Melody to Counterpoint Handoff

**Source engine:** Wayne Shorter (melody). **Target engine:** Bach Counterpoint (counterline).

**Handoff spec:** material melody, overlap_bars 0, transition_type dovetail.

**Result:** Melody engine output ends on bar 8; counterpoint engine receives final phrase as subject, begins on bar 8. No gap. Counterpoint uses melody tail as imitation head.

**Use case:** Big band arrangement; melody hands off to sax section counterline.
