# Example: Melody Over Counterpoint

**Layers:** melody (Shorter), counterpoint (Bach). Both output for bars 5–8.

**Priority spec:** order [melody, counterpoint], tie_resolution first.

**Result:** Melody has precedence. Counterpoint output is reduced (dynamics, register) or deferred to avoid competing with melody. Melody remains primary; counterpoint supports.

**Use case:** Big band: melody in trumpets, counterline in saxes; trumpets take precedence when both active.
