# Example: Motif Fragment — Head Extraction

## Input

Motif: C4–E4–G4–Bb4 (quarter notes, 4 events)
fragment_spec: { head: true, end_index: 2 }

## Parameters

preserve_identity: 0.5

## Output

Fragment: C4–E4–G4 (first three pitches; head of motif)

## Notes

Classic head extraction. The ascending minor third (C–E) and major third (E–G) are preserved. The Bb4 is dropped. The fragment retains the opening contour and intervallic character. Suitable for sequential development or as a call in call-response.
