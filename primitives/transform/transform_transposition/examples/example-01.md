# Example: Motive Transposition by Fifth

**Source:** 4-note motive C4–E4–G4–C5 (ascending arpeggio).

**Transposition spec:** interval 7 (perfect fifth), diatonic false, contour_preserve true.

**Result:** G4–B4–D5–G5. Contour (up-up-up) preserved. Interval classes preserved.

**Use case:** Shorter engine restates head motive at dominant; Bach engine transposes subject for answer.
