# Example: Triadic Interval Mapping

**Source:** 3-note motive C4–E4–G4 (intervals +4, +3).

**Interval profile:** [0, 4, 7] (root, third, fifth).

**Anchor pitch:** G3.

**Result:** G3–B3–D4. Source contour mapped through major triad from new root.

**Use case:** Hill engine restates cell through different harmonic colour; Bach engine applies invertible counterpoint interval rules.
