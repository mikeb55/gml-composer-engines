# Example: Rhythmic Cell Rotation

**Source:** 4-beat rhythm [x . x .] (hits on 1 and 3).

**Rotation spec:** offset 1, direction forward, unit beat.

**Result:** [. x . x] (hits on 2 and 4). Same cell, rotated by one beat.

**Use case:** Stravinsky engine varies accent pattern; jazz engine rotates comping rhythm for variation.
