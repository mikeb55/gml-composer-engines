# Example: Motif Head Extraction

**Source:** 8-note phrase in C minor, bars 1–2.

**Fragment spec:** start_index 0, length 4 events, boundary_type event, contour_preserve true.

**Result:** First 4 notes extracted as head motive. Contour (e.g. up-down-up) preserved. Output references source phrase for provenance.

**Use case:** Wayne Shorter engine extracts head for development; Bach engine extracts subject head for imitation.
