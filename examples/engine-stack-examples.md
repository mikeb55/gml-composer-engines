# Engine Stack Examples

## Stack 1: Full Jazz Big Band

| Engine | Role |
|--------|------|
| Wayne Shorter | Melody, motivic development |
| Andrew Hill | Harmony, intervallic motion |
| Bach Counterpoint | Counterlines, suspensions |
| Stravinsky | Rhythm, accent displacement |
| Ellington | Orchestration, section colour |

Use case: Full arrangement with melody, harmony, counterpoint, rhythmic tension, and orchestration. Conductor coordinates all five. Suitable for complex jazz composition or arranging.

## Stack 2: Chamber / Classical Research

| Engine | Role |
|--------|------|
| Andrew Hill | Harmony |
| Bach Counterpoint | Counterpoint |
| (Chamber orchestration concepts) | Register, voicing |

Use case: Intervallic harmony with Bach-style counterpoint. Smaller ensemble. Focus on harmonic and contrapuntal research without full big band orchestration.

## Stack 3: Colour / Rhythm / Orchestration

| Engine | Role |
|--------|------|
| Messiaen (future) | Colour, mode, harmony |
| Stravinsky | Rhythm, accent |
| Ellington | Orchestration |

Use case: Research into colour (Messiaen modes), rhythm (Stravinsky displacement), and orchestration (Ellington section writing). Melody may come from another source or be minimal. Exploratory stack.
